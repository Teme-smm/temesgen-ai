The Process

How it works

Four simple steps. No video calls if you'd rather not. Just send what you have and we begin.

I

Send a message with your Instagram handle and a short note about your business. That is all I need to start.
II

About me

I'm Temesgen —
I'll handle your social media
so you don't have to.

I am a social media manager based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, working with restaurants, gyms, salons, and local businesses in the US and UK. I use AI-assisted tools to produce content efficiently — which means I can charge a fraction of what an agency charges while still delivering consistent, professional work.

You do not need to organise photo shoots or hire a videographer. You send me whatever you have — photos of your food, your space, your menu, your team. From those I produce posts that feel native to your business. If you have branding or references you like, share them and I will follow them.

I am honest about what social media can and cannot do. It will not double your sales overnight. What it does is build a consistent, professional presence that makes your business look credible and active — which matters when people search for you online.
